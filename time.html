The assignment was to create an animation using Adobe Animate. I loved making my animation even though it wasn’t necessarily a success. The animation is of my raster image: the woman still preoccupied with reading on her lily pad. The vector octopus emerges from behind, but instead of pulling her down into the water, wiggles around playfully. Tragedy was only narrowly avoided because I couldn’t animate the pull-down to my satisfaction. The unintended outcome ended up serving as a suitable metaphor for how, despite my ever-present existential dread, life is still wondrous and there is much joy to be found.
